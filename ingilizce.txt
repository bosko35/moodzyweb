12. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

In order to provide its users with a seamless experience, Moodzy collects only the data that is necessary. Depending on the registration method, the following data is collected:

1.1. Account Creation and Registration Information:

Manual Registration: When the user chooses to register directly via e-mail, we collect Name, Surname, E-mail Address and Password information. Your passwords are stored encrypted using advanced hashing algorithms (e.g., bcrypt/scrypt); no one, including our employees, can access your raw password.

Third-Party Logins (Apple & Google): When you choose to log in to our Platform using your Apple ID or Google Account, we collect the unique user identifier (Token), name-surname and verified e-mail address information provided to us by the relevant organizations. When you register using this method, data sharing takes place to the extent permitted by the privacy settings of the third party.

1.2. Information Collected Automatically:

Device Identifiers: IP address, device type (iPhone/Android), operating system version, browser type and advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID).

Usage Data: Short dramas watched, favorite actions, in-app search queries and content consumption duration.

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

The collected data is processed within the framework of the following legal bases and purposes:

Performance of the Contract: Creation of the user account, providing access to content and crediting purchased virtual products (tokens/coins) to the account.

AI-Powered Personalization: Recommending the most suitable content to you through analysis of your viewing habits.

Platform Improvement: Detection of technical errors, A/B tests and user interface optimization.

Security and Legal Compliance: Prevention of fraudulent activities and misuse of the platform.

Marketing: If you provide consent, sending push notifications and e-mails regarding content and promotions that may interest you.

3. HOW DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?

Moodzy never directly sells user data. Sharing is limited only to the following situations:

Service Providers: Business partners providing data storage (AWS/Google Cloud), customer support systems and data analytics services (AppsFlyer/Firebase).

Advertising Partners: Sharing your advertising identifiers (IDFA/AAID) with advertising networks in anonymized form for advertising targeting purposes.

Group Companies: Affiliates of Moodzy for operational efficiency.

Legal Obligations: When requested by authorized judicial or administrative authorities in accordance with applicable laws (KVKK, LGPD, PDPL, etc.).

4. HOW DO WE STORE AND PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?

4.1. Security: Your data is protected by the AES-256 encryption protocol and monitored against unauthorized access with Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) systems.

4.2. Commitment to Complete Deletion Within 24 Hours: Moodzy respects its users' "Right to Be Forgotten" at the highest level. When a user submits a "Delete My Account" request through the application settings or support channels;

The user's identity, contact information, viewing history and financial records (except invoice records subject to legal retention obligations) are irreversibly deleted from our servers, backups and databases within 24 hours.

Once this process is completed, all digital ties between the user and the platform are terminated.
